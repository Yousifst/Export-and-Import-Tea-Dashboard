# Export-mport-Tea-Dashboard
This dashboard provides an analysis of international trade data, focusing specifically on exports and imports related to tea from specific countries. The data helps identify market trends, key trading partners, and track performance over time.


# Key components and insights from the dashboard:
Total Exported Value: Shows a total exported value of $77.128 billion.
Unit Price Metrics: Displays the average unit price ($6.89K) and median unit price ($5.72K).
Annual Performance: Details last year's performance with a value of approximately $7.42 billion and indicates a 12.55% year-over-year (YTY) change.

# Trend Analysis:
Line graphs illustrate total exported value and unit price trends from 2014 to 2026.
Bar charts show YTY change percentages across different years, highlighting increases and decreases in performance.
#Top Exporters: 
The dashboard identifies top exporters, noting that the selected top 1 country accounts for 23% of total exports.
China is listed as the rank 1 exporter, with an exported value of over $17.7 billion and a global share of nearly 23%.
Sri Lanka is also listed as an exporter/importer partner, which is a major global tea supplier.
Market Insights: The platform helps in making informed decisions, discovering new markets, monitoring competitors, and optimizing pricing and logistics.
